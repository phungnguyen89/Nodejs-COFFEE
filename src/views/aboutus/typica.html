Typica coffee
Subtle sweetness and bitterness

Typica coffee is a kind of Arabica coffee that is 100% Arabica. They're tough to care for and provide modest yields of high-quality coffee beans. Typic is well-known for its subtle sweetness and bitterness, as well as a captivating scent. There are various varieties of the greatest coffee in the world that are all members of the Typical family. Since the 16th century, Yemeni coffee beans have been harvested from the coffee forests of southwestern Ethiopia and transported to the country. Baba Budan received the first Typic seeds from Yemen (India). Later, from 1696 to 1699, the Dutch imported Typica coffee plants from India's Malabar coast to Batavia, which is now known as Jakarta - the capital of Indonesia.
